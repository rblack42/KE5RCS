D_Star Repeater Components
##########################

..  include::   /references.inc

A |DS| repeater normally uses standard Icom_ components. These components include the following:

|DS| Controller
****************

    * ID-RP2C

|DS| Modules
************

One or more of the following:

    * ID-2000V 144MHz Digital Voice repeater

    * ID-RP4000V 440MHz Digital Voice repeater

    * ID-RP2V 1.2GHz Digital Voise repeater

    * ID-RP2D 1.2 GHz Digital Data
